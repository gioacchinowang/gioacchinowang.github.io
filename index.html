Hi there!

I left academia in the autumn of 2021, served as investment director in Qing'an Investment until Nov. 2023. I was a postdoc fellow (working with Prof. Le Zhang and Prof. Pengjie Zhang) in Shanghai Jiao Tong Univ. during 2020, where I join the CMB foreground removal task group for the AliCPT project. I received my doctoral degree in Sep. 2019 from Astroparticle Physics Group (supervised by Prof. Piero Ullio) of SISSA, and later in Dec. I got my second master degree on high-performance computing (supervised by Prof. Luca Heltai) from SISSA and ICTP. Before coming to Trieste, a city of science at the northern Adriatic coast, I studied theoretical physics in Nankai (supervised by Prof. Xinhe Meng) and the TJNU astrophysics center, after graduating from Tianjin Normal University in 2012. I am a member of the IMAGINE consortium since 2017, currently participating the software development and providing technical support for both hammurabi X and IMAGINE. My current research interests include Galactic emissions, CMB foreground removal and cosmic ray propagation. Apart from scientific work and if time permits, I enjoy swimming and cycling.
